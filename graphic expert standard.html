Course Description:

  
Graphic Expert Standard is the most comprehensive diploma course available for students and professionals. 
Offered by the best institute of graphic designing in Delhi, the course focuses on providing extensive training sessions on visual communication designs.


The course is available in classroom teaching and online training mode. 
It can be completed within 6 months in regular, 3 months in fast track or 45 days in crash course duration.


Graphic designing is one of the most popular fields in the multimedia industry. 
It plays an important role in print, electronic media and web designing. 
In order to make students ready for the competitive graphic designing industry, we provide detailed knowledge sessions on the principles and elements of designing. 
In addition, students also get to understand the relevance and application of such principles in the business branding, advertising and web designing industry.
